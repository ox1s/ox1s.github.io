# 1. What did William spend 50 years of his life doing?

* Traveling around the world and enjoying his life.
* ***Running after things that couldn't fill the hole inside him.***
* Spending time with his family and watching his kids grow up.

Ответ:
He spent 50 years running after things that were never going to fill the hole he was trying to fill.

# 2. What happened when William finally got the corner office and people called him "sir"?

* He felt proud and finally happy.
* ***He felt empty and saw another mountain to climb.***
* He immediately retired to spend time with his wife.

Ответ:
He felt empty, describing it as climbing a mountain only to find nothing at the top but another mountain.

# 3. What, according to William, is the difference between his two friends?

* The happy friends have more money and better health.
* ***The happy friends have made peace with themselves and stopped chasing.***
* The happy friends have bigger families and more successful children.

Ответ:
The difference is whether a person has made peace with themselves, stopped chasing, and started just being.

# 4. What did William's wife really want from him?

* A bigger house and nicer cars.
* ***Him to be present, listen, and really be there.***
* Him to work even harder for their future.

Ответ:
She wanted him to be present, to listen, and to actually be there instead of just providing financial things.

# 5. What is "real freedom" according to the text?

* Having a lot of money in the bank.
* ***When negative things don't bother you anymore and you are okay with yourself.***
* Being able to travel and do whatever you want.

Ответ:
Real freedom is when the things that used to bother you do not bother you anymore, and you do not need anything outside of you to be okay inside.

# 6. What is the most important lesson William shares?

* You need to work hard to earn happiness and respect.
* ***You are already enough and can give yourself the feeling of being worthy.***
* Success means climbing higher than your neighbors.

Ответ:
He learned that you are already enough exactly as you are and can give yourself the feeling of being worthy without needing to earn it.

# 7. William says real freedom is when negative things "roll off you like water." What does this mean?

* You take a shower to wash away problems.
* You cry a lot when things go wrong.
* ***Bad things don't affect you emotionally.***

Ответ:
It means that when someone says something nasty to you, it does not bother you or affect you emotionally.

# 8. According to William, what do miserable people in their 80s still do?

* ***They keep comparing themselves to others and keep score.***
* They travel and enjoy retirement.
* They spend all their time with grandchildren.

Ответ:
They are still chasing things, still comparing themselves to other people, and still keeping score.

# 9. What metaphor does William use to describe his feeling of success?

* He felt like he was swimming in money.
* He felt like he had won the lottery.
* ***He felt like he climbed a mountain but found nothing at the top.***

Ответ:
He uses the metaphor of climbing all the way up a mountain and finding nothing there but just another mountain.

# 10. What does the phrase "to keep score" mean in the text?

* To save money for retirement.
* ***To constantly compare yourself to other people.***
* To count points in a sports game.

Ответ:
It means to constantly measure and compare yourself against other people, like his friend who measures himself against a neighbor.
